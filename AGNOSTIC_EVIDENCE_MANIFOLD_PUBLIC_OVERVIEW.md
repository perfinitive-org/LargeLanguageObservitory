# Agnostic Evidence Manifold — Public Overview

Perfinitive builds evidence-backed observatories, registries, and
directories.

These systems organize public sources, claims, policies, changes,
citations, and review status.

Public outputs may include directories, timelines, briefs, watchlists,
and source-backed reports.

The first likely directory surface is AI Vendor Receipts: source-backed
profiles for AI vendor claims, policies, and buyer questions.

The method emphasizes source links, timestamps, review labels,
transparent corrections, and separation between public evidence and
paid analysis.
