# Partner-Facing Preview Checklist

Status: Presentation-readiness checklist for evidence manifold preview material.

## Review Boundary

This checklist reviews presentation readiness only.

It does not certify that ingestion, connectors, dynamic plugins, automated review, or production evidence automation are implemented.

Any page that describes future architecture must clearly distinguish:

- Current
- Implemented differently
- Planned
- Not built

## Preview Posture

Treat any local or development preview as:

- Running and available for inspection.
- Useful for content, navigation, form, and presentation review.
- Not production evidence.
- Not live ingestion-backed infrastructure.

## Content Review Checklist

- The status section matches the actual repository.
- Placeholder content is clearly labeled.
- The site does not imply live ingestion.
- The checklist and docs use the same terminology.
- Partner-facing language does not overstate implementation.
- Future connector, plugin, ingestion, export, or API language is clearly labeled as planned or future.
- Buyer-side AI infrastructure evidence research remains the primary public positioning.
- The trust boundary remains visible: clients do not buy verification, status, badges, rankings, legal advice, or vendor approval.

## Sitemap / Navigation Review

- `/method/evidence-manifold` explains the architecture without replacing the buyer-facing message.
- `/transparency` preserves the commercial and evidence boundary.
- `/custom-research` presents buyer-side research products without checkout, pricing drift, or verification contamination.
